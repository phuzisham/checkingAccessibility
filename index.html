The Center for Global Education offers high impact study abroad programs where internships, undergraduate research and social justice programs provide distinguishable opportunities for you to seize. Study abroad is just one example of how TU can help you write your story.

Global Opportunities

In an increasingly digitally connected world, study abroad has become a key piece of the American college experience. College graduates who possess the personal, academic and professional skills that are gained through international experiences are at the forefront of their fields, especially in competitive job markets.
